IMPORTANCE OF CLEANLINESS

CLEANLINESS IS NOT A WORK WHICH WE DO FORCEFULLY.
IT IS A GOOD HABIT AND HEALTHY WAY OF OUR HEALTHY
LIFE.ALL TYPE OF CLEANLINESS IS VERY NECESSARY FOR
OUR GOOD HEALTH WHETHER IT IS A PERSONAL CLEANLINESS,
AGRICULTURE CLEANLINESS ,ENVIROMENTAL CLEANLINESS .
PET ANIMAL CLEANLINESS OR WORK PLACE CLEANLINESS (LIKE SCHOOL,
COLLEGE,OFFICE ETC.)WE ALL SHOULD BE HIGHLY AWARE ABOUT TO MAINTAIN
CLEANLINESS IN OUR DAILY LIVE.
