INDIAN INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY

THE INDIAN INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY(I.I.Ts) ARE AMONG THE TOP PREMIER INSTITUTE OF ENGINNERING THAT
THAT PROVIDE HIGHER EDUCATION LIKE B.TECH,M.TECH,etc.THESE AUTONOMOUS PUBLIC INSTITUTES ARE ESTABILISHED
UNDER THE INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY ACT,1961 AND ARE CONSIDER AS INSTITUTION OF NATIONAL IMPORTANCE.
